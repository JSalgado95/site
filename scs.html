Student Course Suggestion

Engineering students in the Interamerican University of Bayamon must see a career advisor every semester before they are able to enroll the classes they want to take the next semester. This consumes time off of the professors busy schedule. To reduce the time of this process we will be making an MVC 5 web app that depending on what courses the students have already taken, it will suggest which courses they should take next semester to make the most out of their academic achievements thus simplifying the amount of work the professor must do.
